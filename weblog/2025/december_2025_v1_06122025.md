The '[Swedish Security Service](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Swedish_Security_Service&oldid=1319520963)' (Swedish: Säkerhetspolisen \['sê:kɛrhe:tspu li:sɛn], Säpo \['sě:pu], lit.
